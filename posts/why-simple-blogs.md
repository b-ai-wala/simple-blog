# Why Simple Blogs Are Better

In a world of complex CMSs and heavy frameworks, sometimes all you need is markdown and a simple design.

## Benefits of Simplicity

1. **Fast loading times** - No unnecessary JavaScript or styles
2. **Easy to maintain** - Just markdown files, no database
3. **Version control friendly** - Your posts are just files in git
4. **Focus on writing** - No distractions, just your content

## The Philosophy

> Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.

A blog should be about the content, not the technology. This approach lets you focus on what matters: writing.
